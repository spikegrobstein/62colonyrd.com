Custom built* for the present owner in 1991, this 4+ bedroom, 3 bath Center-Entrance Colonial is set on a ½ acre level lot on a quiet, tree lined street.  It is just steps from the Fiske School and an easy walk to the town center.  The main level’s open floor plan features a large fireplace surrounded by built-in shelves and cabinets in the family room and a china closet in the dining room.  The eat-in kitchen has ample workspace including a granite center counter with sink.  In addition to plenty of cabinet space, there is a walk-in pantry.  French doors in the dining room open out to a screened porch and a bluestone patio in the back of the house.

The specious Master Bedroom on the second floor features an en suite bathroom and two walk-in closets. Three further bedrooms have generous closets and  built-in shelves.  In addition there is  spacious room over the attached garage, suitable as a bedroom, home office, art studio, au pair or in-law suite. It includes a sink and three walk-in closets.

The grounds include a fenced vegetable garden and two Asian pear trees.  Perennial flower beds and mature flowering shrubs surround the house.  A large mulched area in the backyard is perfect for a swing set or picnic table.
