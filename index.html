Custom built* for the present owner in 1991, this 4+ bedroom, 3 bath Center-Entrance Colonial is set on a ½ acre level lot on a quiet, tree lined street. It is just steps from the Fiske School and an easy walk to the town center. The main level's open floor plan features a large fireplace surrounded by built-in shelves and cabinets in the family room and a china closet in the dining room. The eat-in kitchen, updated in 2009, has ample workspace including a granite center counter with a second sink. Fabulous cabinet space and walk-in pantry make this kitchen the chef's dream. French doors in the dining room open out to a screened porch and a bluestone patio in the back of the house.

The spacious Master Bedroom on the second floor features an en suite bathroom and two walk-in closets. Three further bedrooms have generous closets and built-in shelves. In addition there is a large room over the attached garage with a separate staircase from the mud room. It is suitable as a bedroom, home office, art studio, au pair or in-law suite. It includes a sink and three walk-in closets.

The grounds include a fenced vegetable garden and 2 Asian pear trees. Perennial flower beds and mature flowering shrubs surround the house. A large mulched area in the backyard is ready for a swing set or picnic table.

*Designed by Marjorie Pierce, MIT class of 1922.
